# 光的传播

## 瑞利散射

Rayleigh Scattering

描述光线通过比其波长小的微粒时发生的散射现象：
1. 散射强度：$I \propto \frac{1}{\lambda^4}$（与波长的四次方成反比）
2. 典型现象：
   - 天空呈蓝色：短波长蓝光散射更强
   - 日落呈红色：长波长红光穿透性更好
3. 适用条件：微粒直径 $d < \frac{\lambda}{10}$

## 惠更斯原理

Huygens’s Principle: every point on a propagating wavefront serves as the source of spherical secondary wavelets, such that the wavefront at some later time is the envelope of these wavelets.

原理价值：
- 统一解释反射/折射现象
- 奠定波动光学基础

### 反射定律

$$
\theta_i = \theta_r
$$

（入射角等于反射角）

### 折射定律

亦称斯涅尔定律 (Snell's Law)

$$
n_i \sin \theta_i = n_t \sin \theta_t
$$

（$n_i,n_t$为介质折射率）

## 费马定律

Fermat’s Principle

光程定义(Optical Path Length)：

$$
\text{OPL} = \sum_{i=1}^{m} n_i s_i = \int_{S}^{P} n(s) ds
$$

表示光在介质中传播路径在真空中的等效距离

The optical path length corresponds to the distance in vacuum equivalent to the distance traversed ($s$) in the medium of index $n$.

物理意义：

$$
t = \frac{\text{OPL}}{c}
$$

（实际传播时间）

本质是光学版本的"最小作用量原理"

## 电磁学视角

### Waves at an Interface

![fig-4-45](/物理学/电磁学/光学/光的传播/fig-4-45.png)

$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathbf{E}_i &= \mathbf{E}_{0i} e^{i(\mathbf{k}_i \cdot \mathbf{r} - \omega_i t)} \\[8pt]
\mathbf{E}_r &= \mathbf{E}_{0r} e^{i(\mathbf{k}_r \cdot \mathbf{r} - \omega_r t + \varepsilon_r)} \\[8pt]
\mathbf{E}_t &= \mathbf{E}_{0t} e^{i(\mathbf{k}_t \cdot \mathbf{r} - \omega_t t + \varepsilon_t)}
\end{aligned}
$$

(r: reflection, t: transmission)

$$
\mathbf{u}_n \times \mathbf{E}_i + \mathbf{u}_n \times \mathbf{E}_r = \mathbf{u}_n \times \mathbf{E}_t
$$

$$
(\mathbf{k}_i \cdot \mathbf{r}  - \omega_i t) |_{y=b} = (\mathbf{k}_r \cdot \mathbf{r} - \omega_r t + \varepsilon_r) |_{y=b} = (\mathbf{k}_t \cdot \mathbf{r} - \omega_t t + \varepsilon_t) |_{y=b}
$$

对所有时间取值都必须成立，因此

$$
\omega_i = \omega_r = \omega_t
$$

$$
(\mathbf{k}_i \cdot \mathbf{r}) |_{y=b} = (\mathbf{k}_r \cdot \mathbf{r} + \varepsilon_r) |_{y=b} = (\mathbf{k}_t \cdot \mathbf{r} + \varepsilon_t) |_{y=b}
$$

通过调整原点位置 $O$ 使 $\varepsilon_r$ 平衡相位差

$$
[(\mathbf{k}_i - \mathbf{k}_r) \cdot \mathbf{r}]_{y=b} = \varepsilon_r
$$

满足以上条件的所有 $\mathbf{r}$ 构成的平面与 $(\mathbf{k}_i - \mathbf{k}_r)$ 垂直

$$
\mathbf{u}_n \times (\mathbf{k}_i - \mathbf{k}_r) = 0
$$

$$
k_i \sin \theta_i = k_r \sin \theta_r
$$

入射波与反射波在同一介质中，所以 $k_i = k_r$

$$
\boxed{
\theta_i = \theta_r
}
$$

同理

$$
[(\mathbf{k}_i - \mathbf{k}_t) \cdot \mathbf{r}]_{y=b} = \varepsilon_t
$$

$$
k_i \sin \theta_i = k_t \sin \theta_t
$$

又因 $\omega_i = \omega_t$，上式两边同时乘以 $c/\omega_i$ 得

$$
\boxed{
n_i \sin \theta_i = n_t \sin \theta_t
}
$$

### 菲涅斯方程

The Fresnel Equations

#### S-polarization

Senkrecht

![fig-4-47](/物理学/电磁学/光学/光的传播/fig-4-47.png)

如图

$$
\hat{\mathbf{k}} \times \mathbf{E} = v \mathbf{B}
$$

$$
\hat{\mathbf{k}} \cdot \mathbf{E} = 0
$$

Interface 处满足

$$
\mathbf{E}_{0i} + \mathbf{E}_{0r} = \mathbf{E}_{0t}
$$

或

$$
E_i + E_r = E_t \tag{1}
$$

（其中余弦值互相抵消）

假设两者介质的 $\mu_i = (\mu_r) = \mu_r$

$$
- B_i \cos \theta_i + B_r \cos \theta_r = - B_t \cos \theta_t
$$

根据 $\displaystyle B = \frac{E}{v} = \frac{n E}{c}$，有

$$
- \frac{n_i E_i}{c} \cos \theta_i + \frac{n_r E_r}{c} \cos \theta_r = - \frac{n_t E_t}{c} \cos \theta_t
$$

对上式化简

$$
n_i \cos \theta_i (E_i - E_r) = n_t \cos \theta_t E_t \tag{2}
$$

将 $(1)$ 代入 $(2)$ 得

$$
\begin{aligned}
n_i \cos \theta_i (E_i - E_r) &= n_t \cos \theta_t (E_i + E_r) \\[8pt]
(n_i \cos \theta_i - n_t \cos \theta_t) E_i &= (n_i \cos \theta_i + n_t \cos \theta_t) E_r \\[8pt]
\frac{E_r}{E_i} &= \frac{n_i \cos \theta_i - n_t \cos \theta_t}{n_i \cos \theta_i + n_t \cos \theta_t}
\end{aligned}
$$

或

$$
\boxed{
r_{\perp} \equiv \left(\frac{E_{0r}}{E_{0i}}\right)_{\perp} = \frac{n_i \cos \theta_i - n_t \cos \theta_t}{n_i \cos \theta_i + n_t \cos \theta_t}
}
$$

将 $(1) E_r = E_t - E_i$ 代入 $(2)$ 得

$$
\begin{aligned}
n_i \cos \theta_i [E_i - (E_t - E_i)] &= n_t \cos \theta_t E_t \\[8pt]
2 n_i \cos \theta_i E_i &= (n_i \cos \theta_i + n_t \cos \theta_t) E_t \\[8pt]
\frac{E_t}{E_i} &= \frac{2 n_i \cos \theta_i}{n_i \cos \theta_i + n_t \cos \theta_t}
\end{aligned}
$$

或

$$
\boxed{
t_{\perp} \equiv \left(\frac{E_{0t}}{E_{0i}}\right)_{\perp} = \frac{2 n_i \cos \theta_i}{n_i \cos \theta_i + n_t \cos \theta_t}
}
$$

#### P-polarization

Parallel

![fig-4-48](/物理学/电磁学/光学/光的传播/fig-4-48.png)

$$
B_i + B_r = B_t
$$

或

$$
\frac{n_i}{c} E_i + \frac{n_r}{c} E_r = \frac{n_t}{c} E_t
$$

$$
n_i E_i + n_r E_r = n_t E_t \tag{1}
$$

又

$$
E_i \cos \theta_i - E_r \cos \theta_r = E_t \cos \theta_t \tag{2}
$$

将 $\displaystyle (1) E_t = \frac{n_i}{n_t} E_i + \frac{n_r}{n_t} E_r$ 代入 $(2)$，以及 $n_r = n_i, \theta_r = \theta_i$

$$
\begin{aligned}
E_i \cos \theta_i - E_r \cos \theta_i &= \left(\frac{n_i}{n_t} E_i + \frac{n_i}{n_t} E_r\right) \cos \theta_t \\[8pt]
n_t E_i \cos \theta_i - n_t E_r \cos \theta_i &= n_i E_i \cos \theta_t + n_i E_r \cos \theta_t \\[8pt]
(n_t \cos \theta_i - n_i \cos \theta_t) E_i &= (n_i \cos \theta_t + n_t \cos \theta_i) E_r \\[8pt]
\frac{E_r}{E_i} &= \frac{n_t \cos \theta_i - n_i \cos \theta_t}{n_i \cos \theta_t + n_t \cos \theta_i}
\end{aligned}
$$

或

$$
\boxed{
r_{\parallel} \equiv \left( \frac{E_{0r}}{E_{0i}} \right)_{\parallel} = \frac{n_t \cos \theta_i - n_i \cos \theta_t}{n_i \cos \theta_t + n_t \cos \theta_i}
}
$$

将 $\displaystyle (1) E_r = \frac{n_t}{n_i} E_t - E_i$ 代入 $(2)$，以及 $n_r = n_i, \theta_r = \theta_i$

$$
\begin{aligned}
E_i \cos \theta_i - \left( \frac{n_t}{n_i} E_t - E_i \right) \cos \theta_i &= E_t \cos \theta_t \\[8pt]
n_i E_i \cos \theta_i - n_t E_t \cos \theta_i + n_i E_i \cos \theta_i &= n_i E_t \cos \theta_t \\[8pt]
2 n_i \cos \theta_i E_i &= (n_i \cos \theta_t + n_t \cos \theta_i) E_t \\[8pt]
\frac{E_t}{E_i} &= \frac{2 n_i \cos \theta_i}{n_i \cos \theta_t + n_t \cos \theta_i}
\end{aligned}
$$

或

$$
\boxed{
t_{\parallel} \equiv \left( \frac{E_{0t}}{E_{0i}} \right)_{\parallel} = \frac{2 n_i \cos \theta_i}{n_i \cos \theta_t + n_t \cos \theta_i}
}
$$

#### 菲涅尔公式汇总

$$
\begin{aligned}
r_{\perp} &\equiv \left(\frac{E_{0r}}{E_{0i}}\right)_{\perp} = \frac{n_i \cos \theta_i - n_t \cos \theta_t}{n_i \cos \theta_i + n_t \cos \theta_t}\\[8pt]
t_{\perp} &\equiv \left(\frac{E_{0t}}{E_{0i}}\right)_{\perp} = \frac{2 n_i \cos \theta_i}{n_i \cos \theta_i + n_t \cos \theta_t}\\[8pt]
r_{\parallel} &\equiv \left( \frac{E_{0r}}{E_{0i}} \right)_{\parallel} = \frac{n_t \cos \theta_i - n_i \cos \theta_t}{n_i \cos \theta_t + n_t \cos \theta_i}\\[8pt]
t_{\parallel} &\equiv \left( \frac{E_{0t}}{E_{0i}} \right)_{\parallel} = \frac{2 n_i \cos \theta_i}{n_i \cos \theta_t + n_t \cos \theta_i}
\end{aligned}
$$

#### 纯三角函数形式

$$
\begin{aligned}
r_{\perp} &= - \frac{\sin (\theta_i - \theta_t)}{\sin (\theta_i + \theta_t)}\\[8pt]
r_{\parallel} &= + \frac{\tan (\theta_i - \theta_t)}{\tan (\theta_i + \theta_t)}\\[8pt]
t_{\perp} &= + \frac{2 \sin \theta_t \cos \theta_i}{\sin (\theta_i + \theta_t)}\\[8pt]
t_{\parallel} &= + \frac{2 \sin \theta_t \cos \theta_i}{\sin (\theta_i + \theta_t) \cos (\theta_i - \theta_t)}
\end{aligned}
$$

当入射角 $\theta_i$ 趋近于零时

$$
[r_{\parallel}]_{\theta_i = 0} = [-r_{\perp}]_{\theta_i = 0} = \frac{n_t - n_i}{n_t + n_i}
$$

$$
[t_{\parallel}]_{\theta_i = 0} = [t_{\perp}]_{\theta_i = 0} = \frac{2 n_i}{n_i + n_t}
$$

对于所有的 $\theta_i$，有

$$
\begin{aligned}
t_{\perp} + (-r_{\perp}) &= 1 \\[8pt]
t_{\parallel} + r_{\parallel} &= 1
\end{aligned}
$$

def _range(start, stop, step):
    """
    浮点版 range：用整数索引乘以步长生成序列，避免反复 += step 带来的累加误差。
    语义与内置 range 相同，区间为左闭右开 [start, stop)。
    """
    if step == 0:
        raise ValueError("_range() arg 3 must not be zero")
        
    i = 0
    while True:
        current = round(start + i * step, 10)
        
        if step > 0 and current >= stop:
            break
        if step < 0 and current <= stop:
            break
            
        yield current
        i += 1

# ==========================================
# 1. 介质参数（修改此处可切换不同界面与是否出现全内反射）
# ==========================================
# n_i：入射侧介质的折射率（光从该介质射向界面）
# n_t：透射侧介质的折射率（光进入该介质一侧）
#
# 可尝试：
#   - n_i < n_t：光疏 → 光密，观察外反射，R 随入射角增大而升高
#   - n_i > n_t：光密 → 光疏，观察内反射；超过临界角后出现全内反射 (TIR)
#   - 调换 n_i 与 n_t：等价于从另一侧看同一界面，反射/透射角色互换
n_i = 1.0
n_t = 1.5

# ==========================================

print("-" * 70)
print(f"{'入射角(°)':<7} | {'R_s (垂直)':<10} | {'R_p (平行)':<10} | {'R (总平均)':<9} | {'T (总平均)':<9}")
print("-" * 70)

# ==========================================
# 3. 入射角扫描与菲涅尔反射率计算
# ==========================================
# _range(起始角, 终止角, 步长)：左闭右开，单位均为度
# 可尝试：
#   - 缩小步长：在临界角附近细扫，看清 R 陡升或 TIR 出现的转折点
#   - 收窄起止范围：只关注你关心的角度区间，输出更紧凑
for theta_deg in _range(0, 90, 1):
    theta_i = math.radians(theta_deg)
    
    # 斯涅耳定律：sin(θ_t) = (n_i / n_t) · sin(θ_i)
    sin_t = (n_i / n_t) * math.sin(theta_i)
    
    # |sin(θ_t)| > 1 时无实数折射角，发生全内反射；跳过 asin 以免报错
    if sin_t > 1.0:
        print(f"{theta_deg:<10.4f} | {'--- 发生全内反射 (TIR) ---':^53}")
        continue
        
    theta_t = math.asin(sin_t)
    cos_i = math.cos(theta_i)
    cos_t = math.cos(theta_t)
    
    # 菲涅尔公式：r_s 为 s 偏振（电场垂直入射面），r_p 为 p 偏振（电场平行入射面）
    r_s = (n_i * cos_i - n_t * cos_t) / (n_i * cos_i + n_t * cos_t)
    r_p = (n_t * cos_i - n_i * cos_t) / (n_i * cos_t + n_t * cos_i) 
    
    # 能量反射率 R = |r|²；自然光取 s、p 分量的算术平均
    R_s = r_s ** 2
    R_p = r_p ** 2
    R_avg = (R_s + R_p) / 2
    
    # 无吸收时 T = 1 - R（能量守恒）
    T_avg = 1 - R_avg
    
    print(f"{theta_deg:<10.4f} | {R_s:<12.4f} | {R_p:<12.4f} | {R_avg:<12.4f} | {T_avg:<12.4f}")
